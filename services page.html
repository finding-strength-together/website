Forms of Abusive Relationships

If you feel you have been victimized, help and information is available below.
Don't cover up, lie, keep secrets, or protect the problem.
Don't be afraid to tell someone or seek help. Have you experienced this?

Physical Abuse

  • Being held against your will
  • Kept from leaving
  • Being locked out of the house
  • Refusing to help you when you're sick
  • Trauma to your body

For medical services related to Physical Abuse, click here

Emotional Abuse

  • Ignoring your feelings
  • Insulting your values, beliefs, race, heritage, or class
  • Witholding approval or affection as punishment
  • Critizing you
  • Humiliation
  • Refusing to socialize with you
  • Regularly threatening to leave
  • Constantly telling you to leave
  • Destroying household or personal items
  • Deliberately hurting/injuring/killing domestic pets
  • Deprevation of essential personal needs/food/sleep/sanitary items
For services related to Emotional Abuse, click here

Financial Abuse

  • Stealing from you or defrauding you
  • Witholding money to buy food or medical treatment
  • Manipulating or exploiting someone for financial gain
For help related to Financial Abuse, click here

Sexual Abuse

  • Making demeaning remarks
  • Being treated as a sex object
  • Critisizing a person sexually
  • Minimizing the importance of another's feelings about sex
  • Insisting on unwanted touching
  • Withdrawing sex or affection
  • Forcing a person to strip
  • Having affairs after agreeing to monogamy
  • Forced sex
  • Paricularily unwanted sex acts
  • Sex after being beaten
  • Nonconsensual sadistic sexual acts

    Safety Tips
  • Remember to delete your browser history
  • Delete any messages or texts from friends or family that may anger your partner
  • Keep a copy of important papers, money, and some clothes with a friend in case you have to run. (If you have children try to have a favorite toy saved)
  • Confide in a trusted friend-Always tell someone safe
  • Take pictures of any bruises, injuries, or damage of personal belongings, date them and have a trusted friend keep them safe if you do not feel safe enough to go to the police
  • Keep your phone on you or as close by if possible in order to call for help
  • The state has resources such as private mail boxes and ways to hide new addresses. Do not be afraid to reach out.

Mission Statement

Finding Strength Together is on a mission to connect young women effected by domestic violence through shared experiences and guide them to services.

Every survivor is an individual.
Their journey is very personal to their unique circumstances, regardless we are all equally traumatized.
Take some time to thoroughly consider how, as a victim, you were impacted.
Each of our pains and sorrows are our own however we can all join together to share our experiences and gain support and understanding from our sisters in survival.
